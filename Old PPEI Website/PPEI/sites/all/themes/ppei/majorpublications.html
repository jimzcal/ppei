Encouraging Policy Reforms and Improving Governance Processes to Facilitate Timely Release of ENR Revenues

Encouraging Policy Reforms and Improving Governance Processes to Facilitate Timely Release of ENR Revenues to LGUs and to Improve the Poverty Benefits from ENR

The study recommends and encourages reforms in the policy environment for the sharing of revenues from natural resources between the National Government and Local Government Units and for their utilization for poverty alleviation and environmental protection. Revisions to existing joint circulars and departmental memoranda were prepared together with proposed amendments to existing laws.

Advocacy Paper on Increasing Environment and Natural Resources-related Allocation for Poverty Alleviation and Environmental Sustainability

Advocacy Paper on Increasing Environment and Natural Resources-related Allocation for Poverty Alleviation and Environmental Sustainability

The Advocacy Paper lays the technical basis for the conduct of legislative advocacy chiefly for increasing budgets of ENR-related policies, programs and services for poverty reduction in the national budget. The analysis focuses on the 2011-2013 national budget allocations for ENR-related PAPs that can be associated with poverty reduction.

Philippines’ Handbook on Mainstreaming Poverty-Environment Linkages in Local Governance for Inclusive Green Growth

Philippines’ Handbook on Mainstreaming Poverty-Environment Linkages in Local Governance for Inclusive Green Growth

The Poverty-Environment (P-E) Handbook has two purposes. First, it is intended as a supplement to the existing Rationalized Planning System (RPS) which defines the formulation of standard development management documents of the LGU. Particularly, investment programming will be enhanced. Second, the P-E Handbook provides approaches and methodologies as guide for mainstreaming P-E linkages in the LGU governance. Further, the Handbook aims to ascertain that the national-local direction for inclusive green growth is considered in mainstreaming of P-E linkages. It has two parts: (1) Guiding concepts and applications on poverty-environment linkages in LGU governance, and (2) Poverty-Environment Training Manual.
